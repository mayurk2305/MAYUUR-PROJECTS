Career Guidance Information

After completing your education, there are various career paths you can choose based on your interests and strengths. Here are some options:

Diploma Courses

Explore diploma courses in fields like engineering, pharmacy, hotel management, etc.

ITI (Industrial Training Institutes)

Opt for vocational training programs in various trades like electrician, fitter, mechanic, etc.

Arts and Humanities

Pursue courses in humanities, commerce, arts, etc., opening up opportunities in fields like journalism, literature, social work, etc.

Commerce Stream

Choose courses like B.Com, CA, CS, CMA, etc., for a career in commerce and business-related fields.

Science Stream

Opt for courses like PCB, PCM, PCMB, etc., for a career in science and mathematics-related fields.
